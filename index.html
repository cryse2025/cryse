Innovative Design Solutions

For Complex Fractures (High-Tension Types)

We designed a flexible orthopedic mold made from TPU 70A plastic that perfectly fits the fractured limb. This mold is filled with liquid resin that hardens to provide strong, water-resistant support while ensuring comfort and ventilation.

For Simple Fractures or Temporary Immobilization

Our lightweight splint features adjustable fastening systems to adapt to the patient’s morphology, ensuring easy application, removal, and adjustment while maintaining proper immobilization.

Training for Nurses and Caregivers

To guarantee flawless application of our products, we provide specialized training for nurses, including:

  • Theoretical modules on biomechanical principles and device functionality.
  • Hands-on workshops for mastering fitting, adjusting, and removing the devices.
  • Visual guides and video tutorials to support day-to-day use.
  • Ongoing follow-up and support to quickly resolve any application issues.

This dual approach of smart design and comprehensive training ensures safe, comfortable, and effective patient care.
